Experiment 06 · constraint mechanisms @ 32×32 / 10 agents

Connectivity-mechanism shootout @ 32²

Pits the ways of enforcing the comm-graph constraint against each other at the target scale: a soft λ₂ (algebraic-connectivity) penalty, a learned-Lagrangian constraint (RCPO), a PID-Lagrangian controller, and a hard connectivity mask — each run both with explorer/relay roles and with the homogeneous shared policy.

Key finding With explorer/relay roles, the soft λ₂ penalty yields the highest coverage, followed by the learned-Lagrangian (RCPO) and the hard connectivity mask, with the PID-Lagrangian trailing. Roles beat the homogeneous policy under every matched mechanism — how the constraint is imposed and whether the team has differentiated roles both matter, and they compound.
